Phishing Identification Challenge

Phishing is when scammers try to trick you into giving away personal information by pretending to be someone trustworthy. Below are some email examples. Decide if each one is a phishing attempt or legitimate.

Email 1:

From: support@bankofamerica.com

Subject: Urgent: Your Account Has Been Suspended

Dear Customer,

We have detected unusual activity on your account. Click here to verify your identity: Verify Now

Bank of America Security Team

Email 2:

From: amazon-rewards@amazon.com

Subject: You've Won a Free Gift Card!

Congratulations! You've been selected for a $100 Amazon gift card. Claim it now: Claim Gift

Amazon Rewards

Email 3:

From: newsletter@school.edu

Subject: Upcoming Parent-Teacher Conference

Dear Parents,

The next parent-teacher conference is scheduled for May 15th. Please RSVP by clicking here: RSVP

School Administration
