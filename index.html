💡 Quick Cyber Safety Tips

🚫 Never Click Suspicious Links

Hover over links to see where they really go before clicking. Phishing emails often have fake links that look legitimate.

🔐 Use Strong Passwords

Mix upper/lowercase letters, numbers, and symbols. Make them at least 12 characters long. Never reuse passwords!

🛡️ Keep Software Updated

Updates fix security holes. Always update your operating system, browser, and apps when prompted.

👨‍👩‍👧‍👦 Talk to Trusted Adults

If something feels wrong online, tell a parent, teacher, or trusted adult immediately.

🔍 Check Email Senders

Always verify the sender's email address. Scammers often use addresses that look similar to real ones.

🤐 Protect Personal Info

Never share passwords, phone numbers, or addresses with strangers online, even if they seem trustworthy.
