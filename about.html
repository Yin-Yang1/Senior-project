About CyberSafe Kids

This website is a community project designed to teach students and families how to stay safe online. Through interactive tools and educational content, we aim to build confidence in using technology while preparing users to avoid common cyber threats.

Our goal is to make cybersecurity education accessible and engaging for everyone, regardless of age or technical background.

Project Goals:

  • Gather accurate information from trusted cybersecurity sources
  • Design a clear and user-friendly website layout
  • Develop interactive modules for learning
  • Test with students and families for usability

If this project helps even a few people protect their accounts or avoid scams, it will have made a meaningful difference in our community's digital safety.
