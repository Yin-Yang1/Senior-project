📚 Safety Guides to Keep You Safe Online

Learn about common cyber threats and how to protect yourself like a pro! 🦸

🎣 What is Phishing?

Phishing is when scammers try to trick you into giving them your passwords, credit card info, or personal data by pretending to be your bank, a social media site, or your favorite app!

How to Avoid Phishing (Do This!):

  1. 🚫 Don't click on links in emails from people you don't know
  2. 🔍 Check the sender's actual email address (hover over it!)
  3. 👀 Look for spelling errors or weird grammar - that's a red flag!
  4. ⚠️ Beware of urgent language like "ACT NOW!" or "Your account is locked!"
  5. 💻 Use antivirus software with phishing protection
  6. 🤝 When in doubt, ask a trusted adult!

🔒 What is Ransomware?

Ransomware is like a digital lockdown - hackers use bad software to lock up your files and demand money to unlock them. Don't worry though, you can protect yourself!

How to Protect Against Ransomware (Do This!):

  1. ✅ Keep your software and operating system updated (boring but important!)
  2. 📦 Backup your important files regularly to an external drive
  3. 🚫 Don't open attachments from unknown sources
  4. ⚙️ Use reputable antivirus and anti-malware software
  5. 🧐 Be suspicious of unexpected downloads or pop-ups
  6. 👨‍💻 Tell an adult if something looks fishy!

📶 Unsafe Wi-Fi Networks

Free public Wi-Fi sounds great, but hackers LOVE hanging out on public networks to steal data. Here's how to stay safe!

Safe Wi-Fi Practices (Remember These!):

  1. ⚠️ Avoid using public Wi-Fi for banking or entering passwords
  2. 🛡️ Use a VPN (Virtual Private Network) when on public Wi-Fi
  3. 🔒 Look for the lock icon 🔐 in your browser - it means HTTPS is on
  4. ❌ Turn off file sharing when connected to public Wi-Fi
  5. 📵 Turn off auto-connect features on your device
  6. 🏠 When possible, use your mobile data or go home to connect!
